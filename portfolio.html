Infrastructure

Smriti Print House is equipped with state of the art infrastructure, technologies, latest printing machineries & equipments to ensure quality consistent products.

Smriti Print House is an ISO 9001:2015 certified. Smriti Print House Headquarter is spread in an area of 20,000 Sqft. of three storied complex located at Kedia Business Park, Tatibandh, in the capital city of Chhattisgarh state.

We have 1 more fully operational unit (Smriti Galaxy India) which is located at, Jagdalpur city of Bastar district.
