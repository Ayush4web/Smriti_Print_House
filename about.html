About Us

ABOUT US

Welcome TO Smriti Print House, ISO 9001:2015 Certified, established since 1995, as the name signify we are the hub of all printing related works.

Smriti Print House specializes in paper printing, flex printing, signage works, inshop branding, outdoor branding, corporate gift items, All types of Calenders, Diaries, Bags, Pens, Trophies & Packaging works etc.

Smriti Print House is your printing & branding partner, under which you get all kinds of customized printing items & branding solutions according to your business needs or requirements all under one roof.

OUR HISTORY

In 1995 we started our humble journey from Jagdalpur city of Chhattisgarh state. Due to our ideolgy towards the customers, even today, our jagdalpur based Smriti Galaxy India unit is continuously growing ahead with the unwavering trust of our valubale customers.

In the year 2010, we successfully launched our new printing facility and expanded our business at the capital city of chhattisgarh state. In the same year, we started a new venture Prism Signage India Pvt.Ltd.

The year 2019, proved to be very significant to us. This year, Smriti Print House got its new look and setup in the form of new headquarter, spreading in an area of 20,000 sqft. of three storied complex located at Kedia Business Park, Tatibandh, Raipur city of Chhattisgarh state.

VISION

Our vision is to be the most trusted organization and preferred choice amongst customer, for printing and branding services in central India.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide satisfactory products & services from creative designing till delivery, to every customer, on time and in a cost effective manner.

OUR IDEOLOGY

"Customer Satisfaction & Quality Products" are our 2 main objectives and our aim is to constanly strive to fullfill this objective.
